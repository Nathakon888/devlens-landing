FAQ

Common questions

DevLens analyzes publicly available GitHub data: repositories, commit history, language breakdowns, contribution graphs, README files, and organization memberships. We do not access private repositories or any data that isn't already public on GitHub.

Our seniority model correlates with self-reported levels at approximately 80% accuracy for active developers with 10+ public repos. It works best for developers who are consistently active on GitHub. For developers with very few public repos, we flag the confidence level in the response.

Free plans are limited to 10 requests per minute. Growth plans get 60 requests per minute, and Team plans get 200 requests per minute. Monthly lookup limits are separate from rate limits — you can burst up to your rate limit as long as you haven't exhausted your monthly quota.

Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no contracts or cancellation fees. You can cancel directly from your dashboard and your access continues until the end of your current billing period. Unused lookups do not roll over.

DevLens can only analyze public data. If a user has a completely private profile with no public repos, the API will return a minimal profile with whatever public metadata is available (bio, location, organization memberships). We clearly flag when data is limited.
